What is mukbang?


Mukbang is a online audiovisual broadcast in which the host: the mukbanger, eats food. The experience can be an interactive live experience in which the host can interact with a live audience watching the stream or a broadcast experience that only focuses on the host eating his or her meal.


Mukbang started in South Korea and became viral in 2010. Now mukbang experiences can be found anywhere in the world


Here at Mukbang Enthusiasts, we like to focus our content on mostly Korean mukbang experiences rather than ASMR eating videos.
